About The Lost Artifact

The Lost Artifact is an immersive online investigation-based game that challenges participants' general knowledge, web literacy, and problem-solving skills. Dive into a digital treasure hunt filled with clues, mysteries, and hidden puzzles. Will you uncover the artifact?

Format & Winning Criteria

Event Format

  • The event consists of multiple investigative rounds, each presenting a unique challenge.
  • Participants must solve puzzles and move through different levels within a time limit.
  • The game is designed to test logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and research skills.
  • First-come, first-served points distribution applies based on the speed of solving puzzles.

Winning Criteria

  • The participant or team with the highest score at the end of the event wins.
  • Points are awarded based on:
    • Accuracy of answers
    • Speed of completion
    • Complexity of challenges solved
  • In case of a tie:
    • The participant/team that solved the hardest puzzle first wins.
    • If the tie persists, a tie-breaker challenge will be conducted.
